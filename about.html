About Us

Faded Barbershop is your go-to place for precision haircuts and premium barbering services. Founded by Angela and Gloria, we bring years of experience and a passion for styling to every cut.

Our commitment to excellence and customer satisfaction has made us a favorite among locals and visitors alike. We pride ourselves on creating a welcoming atmosphere where you can relax and enjoy a top-notch grooming experience.

At Faded Barbershop, we stay up-to-date with the latest trends and techniques in men's grooming, ensuring that you leave our shop looking and feeling your best. Whether you're looking for a classic cut or a modern style, our skilled barbers have you covered.
